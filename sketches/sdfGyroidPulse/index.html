SDF Gyroid Pulse raymarches a spherical shell carved by a gyroid-like labyrinth, then uses the gyroid signal again to pulse the surface color.

What is happening

The shell distance defines a hollow sphere, while the negative gyroid term removes labyrinth-like channels. Raymarch hits are shaded with two lights and a Fresnel edge glow.

Key variables

Shell radius (1.35)(1.35)(1.35), shell thickness (0.18)(0.18)(0.18), gyroid scale (3.4)(3.4)(3.4), and carve threshold (0.05)(0.05)(0.05) determine how dense and open the labyrinth feels.
