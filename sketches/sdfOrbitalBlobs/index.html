SDF Orbital Blobs raymarches a smoothly blended cluster of six orbiting spheres plus a central sphere, producing a glossy metaball form.

What is happening

Each sphere contributes a signed distance, and smooth-min blending fuses nearby surfaces into a single elastic blob. Normals from the blended field support diffuse, specular, and Fresnel highlights.

Key variables

The blend radii (0.5,0.6)(0.5,0.6)(0.5,0.6) control how gooey the form is, while orbital radius, vertical scale, and animated sphere radius control the cluster motion.
